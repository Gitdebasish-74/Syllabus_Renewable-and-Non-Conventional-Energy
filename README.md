Course Objective:

To know the principles and methods of converting Solar Energy, Wind Energy, Biomass, Geothermal Energy, Ocean Energy, and Hydrogen Energy into usable energy forms, along with their prospects and applications

Course Outcomes: 
COs	CO Statements	Bloom’s level
CO1	Able to understand Renewable and Non-renewable energy sources and their impact on the environment.	L2 – Understand
CO2	Able to analyze the conversion of Solar energy and Wind energy into other forms of energy, and their conversion efficiency	L4 – Analyze.
CO3	Able to analyze the principles, technologies, and applications of Ocean energy sources including Tidal, Wave, and Ocean Thermal Energy Conversion (OTEC).	L4 – Analyze.
CO4	Able to describe the sources, conversion technologies, and applications of Biomass energy and Biofuels.	L2 – Understand
CO5	Able to explain the origin, resources, energy estimation, and applications of Geothermal energy.	L2 – Understand
CO6	Able to explain the principles, technologies, and utilizations of energy from Fuel cell and Hydrogen Gas.	L2 – Understand
